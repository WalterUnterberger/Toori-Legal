Privacy Policy for Toori

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Developer: Walter Unterberger

App name: Toori

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Toori application ("the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the App.

Toori is an educational application designed to support Japanese language learning. Your privacy and data security are taken seriously, and only information necessary to provide the service is collected.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

1. Account Information

When you create an account or log in, the following information may be collected:

  • Email address
  • Authentication credentials managed by Firebase Authentication

Passwords are not stored directly by the App. Authentication is handled securely through Firebase services.

2. Usage and Progress Data

The App stores learning-related information necessary to provide its functionality, including:

  • Learning progress
  • Statistics (such as session counts and performance data)
  • App usage timestamps

This data is used solely to support the learning experience.

3. Technical Information

The App may automatically collect limited technical information required for operation and reliability, including:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash or diagnostic information, if diagnostic services are enabled

How We Use Information

Information collected by the App is used only for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain app functionality
  • To authenticate users and manage accounts
  • To store and display learning progress
  • To improve performance and reliability
  • To diagnose technical issues

The App does not use personal data for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data Storage and Security

User data is stored securely using Firebase services and local device storage technologies.

Security measures include:

  • Encrypted network communication (HTTPS)
  • Secure authentication mechanisms
  • Controlled access to stored data

Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are implemented to protect user information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

Data Sharing

The App does not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

Information may be processed by trusted service providers strictly for operating the App, including:

  • Firebase (authentication and backend services)

These services operate under their own privacy and security obligations.

Data may also be disclosed where required by law.

Data Retention

User data is retained only as long as necessary to provide app functionality or comply with legal obligations.

Users may request deletion of their account and associated data at any time.

User Rights

Depending on applicable laws, users may have the right to:

  • Access personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of data
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for general audiences and educational use. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate parental consent where required by law.

Third-Party Services

Toori uses Firebase services provided by Google LLC.

Firebase privacy information: Firebase Privacy and Security

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date.
