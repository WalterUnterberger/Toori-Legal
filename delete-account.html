Delete Your Toori Account

Delete Directly in the App

Toori users can delete their account directly inside the application.

Navigate to:

Profile → Settings → Delete Account

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • User authentication account
  • Registered email address
  • User profile information
  • Synced educational progress data

Alternative Deletion Request

If you cannot access the application, you may request deletion by email:

w.unterberger.dev@gmail.com

Processing Time

Deletion requests are typically processed within 7 business days.
