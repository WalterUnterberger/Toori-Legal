Delete Your Toori Account

Last updated: May 25, 2026

App name: Toori

Developer: Walter Unterberger

Delete Directly in the App

Toori users can delete their account directly within the application.

Profile → Settings → Delete Account

This is the fastest and recommended method for account deletion.

What Will Be Deleted

When your Toori account is deleted, the following account-associated data will be removed:

  • User authentication account
  • Registered email address
  • User profile information
  • Firebase user identifier associated with the account
  • Synced learning progress associated with the account

Locally Stored Data

Some learning-related data may also be stored locally on your device.

Removing the Toori application from your device may also remove locally stored app data, depending on your device settings and Android system behavior.

Alternative Deletion Request

If you cannot access the application, you may request account deletion manually by email.

w.unterberger.dev@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your Toori account so the request can be identified and processed correctly.

Processing Time

Manual deletion requests are typically processed within 7 business days.
