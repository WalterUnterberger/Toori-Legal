Delete Your Toori Account

Last updated: May 14, 2026

App name: Toori

Developer: Walter Unterberger

Delete Directly in the App

Toori users can delete their account directly inside the application.

Navigate to:

Profile → Settings → Delete Account

What Will Be Deleted

When you delete your Toori account, the following account-associated data will be deleted:

  • User authentication account
  • Registered email address
  • User profile information
  • Firebase user identifier associated with the account
  • Synced educational progress data associated with the account

Locally Stored Data

Some learning data may be stored locally on your device. Removing the App from your device may also remove locally stored app data, depending on your device and operating system settings.

Alternative Deletion Request

If you cannot access the application, you may request deletion by email:

w.unterberger.dev@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your Toori account so the request can be identified and processed.

Processing Time

Deletion requests are typically processed within 7 business days.
