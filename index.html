About Toori

Toori is a Japanese language learning application focused on kana, kanji, vocabulary study, dictionary functionality, and learning progress tracking.

This site exists to make Toori's privacy practices, account deletion process, and third-party licensing obligations publicly accessible.

Repository Scope

This public site is used for legal and compliance information only. It does not contain the Toori application source code.

Where required, this site may provide access to adapted third-party datasets used by Toori under their respective license terms.
