Overview

This page documents externally licensed datasets used by Toori and provides attribution, licensing details, and access where required.

Some Japanese language data used in Toori may be derived from third-party datasets. Such data remains subject to the license terms of the original providers.

JMdict / JMdictDB

Japanese Dictionary Data

Japanese dictionary data used in Toori may be derived from JMdict, provided by the Electronic Dictionary Research and Development Group (EDRDG).

  • Dataset: JMdict / JMdictDB
  • Provider: Electronic Dictionary Research and Development Group
  • License: Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International

Original source: EDRDG

License details: EDRDG License Page

Data used in Toori may have been parsed, filtered, normalized, restructured, or converted into SQLite database format for application use.
